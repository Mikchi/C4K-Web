What is CardzForKiz H.A.?

Cardz For Kidz Hacienda Heights is a branch of the organization Cardz For Kidz. Cardz For Kidz is an organization dedicated to uplifting spirits across the globe by delivering inspiring handmade cards.

For more info, visit the official CardzForKidz website here!!

What We Do

  • Make personalized greeting cards for those in need.
  • Share hope and encouragement through creative designs.
  • Craft each card with care to brighten someone's day.

How it Works

Join our Discord Server!

All information about our events, theme, meetings, and community updates will be shared there.

How it goes

At the start of each event, we’ll announce a theme. Members will create handmade cards based on that theme—using English or another language they know.

All done from here

After finishing your cards, give them to your school rep to send to the president for shipping. If your school isn't local, please ship your cards yourself.
